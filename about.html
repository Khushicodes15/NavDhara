About Us

Our platform reimagines Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) by leveraging Artificial Intelligence to deliver fast, reliable, and actionable sustainability insights. It goes beyond traditional analysis by predicting the environmental footprint of materials and processes — from carbon emissions to energy consumption and water usage. With intuitive visualization and AI-driven intelligence, we make complex assessments simple, empowering industries to make smarter, greener, and more circular decisions.

This initiative is proudly aligned with the vision of the Ministry of Mines, Government of India, supporting the nation’s drive toward sustainability and resource efficiency.
